Project Name
LearnSync — Academic OS for High-Performers

Problem Statement
Students frequently navigate unstructured study sessions without timely, constructive feedback, leading to inefficiency and frustration. Simultaneously, educators operate with "instructional blindness," lacking real-time visibility into specific student friction points. This disconnect allows critical learning gaps to persist and widen, hindering overall academic progress.

Project Description
LearnSync is a comprehensive cognitive ecosystem designed to optimize the learning lifecycle for both students and educators. The platform features a "Monk Mode" focus environment equipped with a Socratic, non-linear AI tutor and an adaptive, AI-managed task system to eliminate planning paralysis. For educators, LearnSync provides "Red Zone" friction heatmaps, transforming raw student struggle data into actionable classroom signals for targeted, high-impact intervention and curriculum adjustment.

How Google AI Was Used

Socratic Scaffolding: Leveraging Gemini’s multimodal capabilities to analyze student queries and provide guided hints and conceptual frameworks. The AI is prompted to provide "scaffolding" rather than direct answers, fostering independent problem-solving skills.

Instructional Insight Extraction: Utilizing AI to aggregate and categorize individual student doubts. The system groups common friction points into high-level themes, providing teachers with immediate, actionable data on where the cohort is struggling.

Dynamic Planning and Prioritization: Employing AI to deconstruct complex syllabi and personal goals into structured, prioritized task lists. This reduces the student’s cognitive load by automating the transition from "what to study" to "how to study."
